FAQ

Common Questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often.

How quickly will I hear back?
+
We respond to all inquiries within 24 hours on business days. For urgent matters, calling us directly is the fastest way to get help.
Is my information secure?
+
Absolutely. All data submitted through our forms is encrypted and handled in compliance with federal regulations. We never sell or share your personal information.
Can I check on my case status?
+
Yes! Current clients can log into their portal to track progress in real time, or call us directly for a status update from your assigned specialist.
Do you offer free consultations?
+
Yes, we offer a free credit audit for all new clients. This gives you a clear picture of where you stand and what we can do to help improve your score.
How do I become a partner?
+
Visit our Partner Program page to learn about commission structures and apply. Most partners are approved within 24 hours.
