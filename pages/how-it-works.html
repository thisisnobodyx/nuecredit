FAQ

Your Questions, Answered

Everything you need to know about the nueCredit process.

How long does it take to see results?
+
Most clients see their first items removed within 30–45 days. On average, our clients gain 73 points within 90 days. Results depend on the complexity of your credit profile, but we work aggressively from day one.
Is credit repair legal?
+
Absolutely. Under the Fair Credit Reporting Act (FCRA) and the Credit Repair Organizations Act (CROA), you have the legal right to dispute inaccurate, outdated, or unverifiable information on your credit reports. nueCredit follows all federal and state regulations.
What items can you dispute?
+
We dispute late payments, collections, charge-offs, bankruptcies, repossessions, foreclosures, inquiries, and any other items that are inaccurate, outdated, or unverifiable. If it's hurting your score and shouldn't be there, we challenge it.
Can I cancel anytime?
+
Yes. There are no long-term contracts. You can cancel your subscription at any time with zero penalties. We believe in earning your trust monthly through results, not locking you into commitments.
How is nueCredit different from other companies?
+
We combine AI-powered credit analysis, 3-bureau monthly disputes, real-time tracking, and dedicated human support. Most competitors only dispute with one bureau or don't provide live tracking. We do both — and more — starting at just $79/month.
Do I need all three bureau reports?
+
For the best results, yes. Each bureau may have different information, and lenders check different ones. Our Pro and Premium plans dispute all three bureaus every month. The Basic plan lets you choose one bureau to start with.
