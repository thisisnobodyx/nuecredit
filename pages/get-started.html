FAQ

Getting Started Questions

Everything you need to know before signing up.

How long does it take to see results?
+
Most clients see their first improvements within 30–60 days. The full credit restoration process typically takes 4–6 months depending on the complexity of your case.
Will this hurt my credit score?
+
No. Filing disputes is a legal right under the Fair Credit Reporting Act (FCRA). The dispute process does not negatively affect your credit score. In fact, removing inaccurate items typically improves it.
Can I cancel at any time?
+
Yes. There are no contracts or long-term commitments. You can cancel your subscription at any time from your client portal or by contacting our support team.
What if I don't know my credit score?
+
No problem. Once you sign up, we'll pull your credit reports from all three bureaus as part of our analysis. We'll give you a complete picture of where you stand and what we can improve.
Is there a guarantee?
+
While no credit repair company can guarantee specific results (that would actually be illegal under CROA), our track record speaks for itself. Our average client sees a 73-point improvement, and we have a 99% satisfaction rate.
