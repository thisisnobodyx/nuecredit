Credit Repair Organizations Act (CROA) Disclosure

Under the Credit Repair Organizations Act (CROA), you have the right to dispute inaccurate information in your credit report by contacting the credit bureau directly. You are not required to use a credit repair organization to exercise this right.

nueCredit cannot guarantee specific results. No one can lawfully remove accurate and timely negative information from your credit report. You have the right to obtain a copy of your credit report from a credit bureau.

You have three business days to cancel this contract without penalty. If you cancel, any payment you've made will be returned to you within five business days.

Service Agreement

By enrolling in nueCredit's credit restoration service, you authorize nueCredit to act on your behalf in communicating with credit bureaus, creditors, and other entities for the purpose of investigating and disputing inaccurate, incomplete, or unverifiable information on your credit reports.

You understand that results vary based on individual credit profiles and that nueCredit does not guarantee any specific outcome. Your subscription may be canceled at any time without penalty.
