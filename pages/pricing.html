FAQ

Pricing Questions

What's included in the enrollment fee?
+
The one-time enrollment fee covers your initial credit report analysis, account setup, dispute strategy creation, and first round of dispute letters. It's a one-time charge — you won't see it again.
Can I switch plans later?
+
Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. If you upgrade, the new rate starts on your next billing cycle. If you downgrade, we'll apply the lower rate immediately.
How long should I stay enrolled?
+
Most clients see significant results within 3–6 months. The bureau dispute process takes 30–45 days per round, so we recommend at least 3 months for meaningful improvement. That said, you can cancel anytime.
Is there a money-back guarantee?
+
We don't offer money-back guarantees because credit repair is a process, not a one-time event. However, we never lock you in — cancel anytime if you're not satisfied with our service.
