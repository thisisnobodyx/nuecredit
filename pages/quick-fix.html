Quick Fix FAQ

Common questions about our VIP express service.

How fast is Quick Fix?
+
Most Quick Fix cases are completed within 2–4 weeks. The exact timeline depends on the complexity of your credit report and the number of items that need to be addressed.
How much does Quick Fix cost?
+
Quick Fix pricing is custom — we review your credit report and provide a quote based on the work needed. Every case is different, so we tailor the price to your specific situation.
Can I pay in installments?
+
Yes. You can pay the full quote upfront or split it into 2 payments. The first payment is due before work begins, and the second is due at the midpoint of your case.
What's the difference between Quick Fix and monthly plans?
+
Our monthly plans are ongoing subscription-based services for gradual credit improvement. Quick Fix is a one-time, accelerated service with a dedicated VIP specialist and aggressive dispute strategy — designed to deliver results in 2–4 weeks instead of months.
Do you guarantee results?
+
Per the Credit Repair Organizations Act (CROA), no credit repair company can guarantee specific results. However, our Quick Fix service uses the most aggressive dispute strategies available, and our VIP team has extensive experience delivering fast results.
